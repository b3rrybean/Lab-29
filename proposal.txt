Outline:
Attendance patterns at San Jose Sharks hockey games!
Lists
1. Season ticket holders
2. Casual fans
3. First-time fans
Elements
1. Recent promotion (increase)
2. Winning streak (increase)
3. Bad weather (decrease)
4. First-time fans switch to casual fans (change)
Time period
25 game nights

Project Overview:
My project creates a simulation for attendance patters at San Jose Sharks hockey games.
This software will illustrate the potential impact that different factors like recent promotions, 
bad weather, and winning streaks will have.
